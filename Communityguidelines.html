Our Community Guidelines

Welcome to our text-sharing platform! We're thrilled to have you here. To ensure a positive and respectful environment for everyone, we've established these guidelines. Please take a moment to review them.

---

General Principles

  • Respectful Interaction: Treat all members with respect, even if you disagree with their views. Healthy discussions are encouraged, but personal attacks and harassment are not.
  • No Illegal Content: Do not post or share any content that is illegal, promotes illegal activities, or is otherwise in violation of local or international laws.
  • Privacy Matters: Do not share private or confidential information about yourself or others without explicit consent.
  • Authenticity: Do not impersonate other individuals or entities. Be truthful about your identity and intentions.
  • No Spam or Commercial Solicitation: Do not use the platform for unsolicited advertising, promotional materials, or any form of commercial spam.
---

Content Guidelines: Understanding "Notes"

Our platform allows for a unique feature: personal "notes." We understand that these notes can sometimes be a space for unfiltered expression. Here's how we handle certain types of language within them:

Important Note on Language in Personal Notes:

We permit the use of cussing, strong language, and even slurs within your personal notes, PROVIDED THAT IT IS NOT DIRECTED TOWARDS ANOTHER INDIVIDUAL OR GROUP. This means:

  • Using such language for personal expression, comedic effect, or to convey intense emotion within your own thoughts is generally acceptable.
  • However, any language, including cussing or slurs, that is used to harass, threaten, abuse, defame, or directly target another user or group will result in immediate action. This is a zero-tolerance policy.
---

Prohibited Actions & Content (Platform-Wide)

  • Harassment and Bullying: Any form of harassment, bullying, stalking, or intimidation directed at other users.
  • Hate Speech: Content that promotes discrimination, hatred, or violence against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic.
  • Threats of Violence: Any credible threats of harm or violence towards others, including self-harm.
  • Child Exploitation: Any content related to child sexual abuse or exploitation is strictly prohibited and will be reported to the appropriate authorities.
  • Graphic Content: Excessive or gratuitous violence, gore, or sexually explicit material not relevant to artistic or educational purposes.
---

Reporting Violations

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it immediately using our reporting tools. Your reports help us keep the community safe and positive for everyone.

---

Consequences of Violations

Violations of these guidelines may result in various actions, including:

  • Content removal
  • Temporary suspension of your account
  • Permanent ban from the platform
  • Reporting to law enforcement, if applicable

By using this platform, you agree to abide by these Community Guidelines. Thank you for helping us build a vibrant and respectful community!
